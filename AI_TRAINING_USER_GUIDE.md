## What Can You Train?

The AI learns from **4 types of knowledge**:

### 1. 📬 Example Nudges
**What**: Real examples of good messages that worked well  
**Why**: AI learns your communication style and patterns  
**Example**:
- Title: "Follow-up on delayed dispatch"
- Content: "Ravi, the Sharma Industries dispatch — has it been raised? Confirm by EOD."

### 2. 📦 Product Info
**What**: Product knowledge, features, use cases, target customers  
**Why**: AI understands your products better and can suggest relevant cross-sells  
**Example**:
- Title: "Dr Bio Compostable Range"
- Content: "Dr Bio is our biodegradable packaging line. Includes dunnage, bags, films. ESG-focused. Target: logistics, FMCG, exporters with sustainability mandates."

### 3. 📖 Terminology
**What**: Company-specific terms, abbreviations, internal jargon  
**Why**: AI uses your company's language correctly  
**Example**:
- Title: "LTV Meaning"
- Content: "LTV = Lifetime Value. Annual revenue from customer. ₹20L+ = key account."

### 4. 📋 Guidelines
**What**: Communication rules and best practices  
**Why**: AI follows your standards consistently  
**Example**:
- Title: "Deadline Format"
- Content: "Always end nudges with clear deadline: 'Confirm by EOD', 'Update by tomorrow', 'Revert by 6pm'."

## How to Add Knowledge

### Step 1: Click "+ Add Knowledge" Button
Located at the top right of the Knowledge Base section.

### Step 2: Fill in the Form

**Category** (Required)
- Choose: Example Nudge, Product Info, Terminology, or Guideline

**Title** (Required)
- Short, descriptive title (e.g., "Follow-up on pricing")

**Content** (Required)
- The actual knowledge/example
- Can be multiple lines
- Be specific and clear

**Language** (Optional)
- Choose which language this applies to
- "All Languages" = applies to everyone
- Specific language = only for reps using that language

**Priority** (1-10)
- How important is this knowledge?
- 10 = Most important (AI will prioritize this)
- 5 = Normal importance
- 1 = Least important

### Step 3: Click "Save Entry"
Done! The AI will now use this knowledge when generating nudges.

---

## Managing Knowledge Entries

### Edit an Entry
- Click the ✏️ (pencil) icon next to any entry
- Update the information
- Click "Update Entry"

### Deactivate/Activate
- Click the 👁️ (eye) icon to temporarily disable an entry
- AI won't use deactivated entries
- Click again to reactivate

### Delete an Entry
- Click the 🗑️ (trash) icon
- Confirm deletion
- Entry is permanently removed

## Best Practices

### 1. Start with Examples
Add 5-10 real examples of good nudges that worked well. The AI learns fastest from examples.

### 2. Be Specific
Instead of: "Follow up with customer"  
Write: "Ravi, the Asian Polytech discount discussion — what was their final ask? Update by EOD."

### 3. Use High Priority for Important Rules
Set priority 8-10 for:
- Critical communication guidelines
- Key product information
- Important terminology

### 4. Keep It Updated
- Add new examples when you write a particularly good message
- Update product info when launching new products
- Add new terminology as your company evolves

### 5. Language-Specific Examples
If you have reps using different languages (Hinglish, English), add examples for each language so the AI learns the right tone.

## How AI Uses Your Knowledge

When generating a nudge, the AI:
1. ✅ Looks at the rep's language preference
2. ✅ Fetches top 10 most relevant knowledge entries
3. ✅ Groups them by category
4. ✅ Learns patterns from your examples
5. ✅ Applies your product knowledge
6. ✅ Uses your terminology correctly
7. ✅ Follows your guidelines

**Result**: More personalized, accurate, company-specific nudges!

## Troubleshooting

**Q: I added knowledge but AI isn't using it**
- Check if the entry is Active (not deactivated)
- Make sure priority is at least 5
- Try adding more examples in the same category

**Q: Can I add the same knowledge in multiple languages?**
- Yes! Add separate entries for each language
- Or use "All Languages" if it applies to everyone

**Q: How many entries should I have?**
- Minimum: 10-15 entries to see good results
- Recommended: 30-50 entries for excellent results
- No maximum - add as much as you want!

**Q: Can multiple people add knowledge?**
- Yes! Anyone with access to the app can add/edit knowledge
- All changes are saved immediately
